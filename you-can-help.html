How You Can Help

Donate to us

The most direct way to help us is to donate on our donate page. Your donations help us to invest in products and help to run projects to clean our oceans and help keep them clean. Your money also helps us to keep educating businesses and schools, ensuring that we can spread our message to as many people as possible. You can set up monthly payments, or just give a one-time donation. Please give what you can.

Volunteer

Along with a few other charities we help to organise events for the ‘Big Atlantic Clean-up’ right here in the UK. Through the hard work of our dedicated volunteers we helped to organise hundreds of events in dozens of counties in England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland. Visit our volunteer co-ordinator’s website for more information!
