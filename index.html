Polluted Waters

Our oceans are facing a crisis, one that we created and one that only we can fix. Plastic. The material that has made most of our modern life possible is now flooding into our oceans causing an ecological disaster. Every year millions of tons of plastic end up in our seas. Poisoning marine life in devastating numbers. Unless we change the way we view and use plastic in our day to day lives, our oceans will be filled with plastic, and nothing else.

Drowning in Plastic

As we pollute the ocean with plastic we endanger the lives of all manner of sealife. Infamously turtles are some of the worst effected, with all 7 species known to ingest plastic. Hatchlings have been known to get tangled up in plastic on the way from the beaches and becoming tangled in it can cause adults to require amputations. Some seabird chicks can have a bodyweight of up to 15% plastic. And, of the 90 known species of whales, 47 of them are known to ingest plastics, 27 species are known to become entangled in plastic. In one case 88lbs of plastic was found in the stomach of a juvenile Cuvier Beaked whale.
