What We're Doing to Help

Science

Since our company’s inception we’ve known how important it is to be in close contact with scientist and the scientific community. As long as we can stay up to date on the latest scientific research and discoveries, then we can implement new and improved ways to help solve the crisis. And apply the knowledge to our Education Team and ensure that we are providing the most updated information. Knowledge and information are key to ensuring everyone is aware of the problem and knows how to help work towards the solution.

Education

Our Education Team is the pride and joy of Living Ocean Trust. We are the only charity to work directly with a team of scientists to provide cutting edge information in the most accessible way possible. We lead talks for businesses to help them reduce their plastic waste, provide online resources, and have spokespeople to help schools inform the next generation. We have also worked closely with headteachers and governing bodies to help integrate cutting edge science into the curriculum at all age levels.

Cleaning Up

Although it is helpful to gather information and provide resources to others, we here at the Living Ocean Trust believe in taking action ourselves. We are partnering with a number of supermarkets nationwide to minimise their use of plastics in packaging and use of plastic bags. In partnership with the ‘Big Atlantic Clean-up’ we’re out in the ocean every month cleaning up as much plastic as we can, and investing in technology to help us move away from our dependency on plastic altogether.
